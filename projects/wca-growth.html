Competitor base over time

Here's a visual representation of how the number of individual competitors has grown over time. The growth has been exponential, with the exception of the COVID-19 pandemic that made the growth stagnate completely for a while. Note: 1982 World Championship is excluded from the table for conviniency reasons but the 1982 competitors are included in the data.

Number of competitions per year

We can see a similar trend here. The number of WCA-sanctioned competitions grew rapidly until 2020 when the pandemic hit.

Map with some competition and competitor data

The countries are colored based on how long ago the first competition in that country was. We can see that the competitions have been around in Europe and North America the longest. Africa has had competitions in very small portion of the countries, but besides that, the WCA has expanded very effectively worldwide.

The numbers tell the number of individual people that have competed in that country. The largest competitor bases can be found in the USA and China.
